The **Ryūsei Shogunate** (流星幕府, _Ryūsei Bakufu_) is a sovereign state located in the south of
the continent of [Elysium](Elysium). It is a [feudal](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Feudalism)
[military dictatorship](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Military_Dictatorship), centered on the figure
of [shogun](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shogun) [Niszara](Niszara_Ryūsei), leader of the Ryūsei
dynasty. Its capital is the city of Tenshikyo.

Composed of a small population that is, at the same time, highly hierarchized, the Shogunate
combines martialist traditions with religious elements of [Madeism](Madeism), which exerts strong
influence over civil and military administration. Power is rigidly distributed through bonds of
personal loyalty between the shogun and her vassals, among whom the
[Tairō](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tair%C5%8D) stands out, currently Saito Ryūsei.

# Etymology

The name “Ryūsei Shogunate” was adopted since the founding of the regime under the leadership of
shogun Niszara in reference to her established dynasty. “Shogunate” derives from the Japanese
_shogun_ (将軍), a historical title for military commanders, while _“Ryūsei”_ (流星) means “shooting
star” or “meteor.”
